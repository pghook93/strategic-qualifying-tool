Strategic Intake

Paste policy text or attach documents for strategic analysis. Scoring is strictly in 5-point increments against the Three-Pillar framework.

Scoring Mechanics

Scoring Scale (5-point increments only)

Each sub-value domain is given a score out of 50 based on the uploaded document's alignment with Organisation X's values and strategy.

50
Exceptional. Policy explicitly and primarily focuses on the criterion.
40
Strong. Specific, actionable provisions that strongly support the criterion.
30
Moderate. Mentions or indirectly supports, but lacks enforcement.
20
Weak. Vague lip service or mixed impacts.
10
Neutral. Does not meaningfully address the criterion.
0
Conflict. Actively undermines or contradicts the criterion.

Recommendation Thresholds

The total alignment score determines the recommended level of strategic engagement and submission effort.

Lead · 85–100
Draft comprehensive standalone submission and seek a public hearing.
Partner · 60–84
Support a sector-wide response (e.g. via VCOSS or peak bodies); provide targeted data only.
Monitor · < 60
Track via Evidence Sentinel. Do not allocate staff hours.

Effort Estimate

Reflects the expected staff and resource burden required to meet the recommended action.

High
Complex, new data collection, multiple staff.
Medium
Moderate research; synthesis of existing positions.
Low
Minimal synthesis or simple endorsement.
Unknown
Mandatory fallback if inputs lack sufficient information.
